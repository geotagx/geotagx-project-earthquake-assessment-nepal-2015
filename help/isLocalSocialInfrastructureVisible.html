In this question we are trying to identify photos showing any form of local social infrastructure in a Nepali village. When you look for social infrastructure, you should refer to any of the assets that accommodate social services such as education facilities, healthcare facilities, public spaces and buildings. Some examples of social infrastructure may include children's residential, school, market, government office, hospital, prisons, community housing, bakeries and shops, public transport, etc.
