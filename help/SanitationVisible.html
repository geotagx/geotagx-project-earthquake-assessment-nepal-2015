In this question we are trying to identify photos showing any sort of sanitation or hygiene issue in a Nepali village or displacement. As explained by WHO, the word ’sanitation’ generally refers to the provision of facilities and services for the safe disposal of human urine and faeces, as well as the maintenance of hygienic condition, through services such as garbage collection and wastewater disposal, and practices such as hand-washing with soap at critical times (after defecating and before eating or preparing food). Maintaining basic hygiene behaviour and adequate sanitation is fundamental for improving health and reduce the incidence of some diseases, such as diarrhoea, pneumonia, scabies, and infections. (Source.)
