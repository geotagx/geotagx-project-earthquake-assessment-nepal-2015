In this question we are trying to identify photos showing any sort of sanitation issue in a Nepali village or displacement. As explained by WHO (http://www.who.int/topics/sanitation/en/), the word sanitation generally refers to the provision of facilities and services for the safe disposal of human urine and faeces, as well as the maintenance of hygienic condition, through services such as garbage collection and wastewater disposal. Inadequate sanitation is a major cause of disease world-wide and improving sanitation is known to have a significant beneficial impact on health both in households and across communities.

Examples of when you should select include photos showing any evidence of sewage or garbage piling up in the streets, human waste, scarce or damaged toilets facilities, or population using shared non-emergency toilets.
