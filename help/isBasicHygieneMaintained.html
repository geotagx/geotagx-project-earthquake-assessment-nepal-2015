In this question we are trying to identify photos showing any kind of hygiene issues which would be relevant to the application. Maintaining basic hygiene is fundamental for improving health. In particular, hand-washing with soap at critical times (after defecating and before eating or preparing food) can significantly reduce the incidence of some diseases, such as diarrhoea, pneumonia, scabies, and infections. (source http://www.unicef.org/wash/index_hygiene.html)

Examples of when you should click are photos showing a population maintaining basic hygiene, which may include handwashing with soap practice or using soap to wash themselves or clothes, population accessing water from container with lid or safe water in general, household with toilets.
