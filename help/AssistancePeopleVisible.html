In this instance we are trying to identify photos showing people requiring assistance and support in order to assess their safety and security. To answer this question you should look for people in need of different forms of assistance and for photos showing any gap in measures that ensure safety and security for displaced people.

Examples of when you should click include photos showing people requiring food, cash, WASH (water, latrines, etc.), shelter, and health and nutrition items.

You should also click if you see any evidence of hazardous material contamination or waste that pose potential threats to public health or the environment, as well as evidence of people breaking the law.
